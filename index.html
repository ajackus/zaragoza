Our Story

A Taste of Spain,
Heart & Soul

Born from a love of Spanish tradition and the vibrant culture of Zaragoza, our restaurant brings the spirit of the Iberian table to life — fresh ingredients, time-honored techniques, and a warm, convivial atmosphere where every meal becomes a memory.

Our chef brings over two decades of Andalusian and Aragonese culinary expertise, curating a menu that celebrates the regional diversity of Spain's most beloved dishes.
