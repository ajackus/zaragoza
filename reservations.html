Good to Know

Reservation Policies

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made more than 4 hours before your reservation will incur no charge. Later cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a $25-per-person fee.

Large Groups

For groups of 8 or more, please contact us directly at +1 (212) 555-0182 or via our events enquiry form to discuss availability.

Table Hold Time

We hold reserved tables for 15 minutes past the booked time. Please call us if you're running late and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Dietary Requirements

We cater for a wide range of dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and major allergens. Please note these in your reservation.
